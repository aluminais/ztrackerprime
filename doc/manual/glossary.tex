\chapter{Glossary}
\begin{itemize}
\item [\emph{tick}] A tick is when the player moves to the next row.
\item [\emph{subtick}] There are many subticks per tick.  How many?
\item [\emph{player command}] A special instruction to the player. Has the form \pc{Xyyzz}.
\item [\emph{hexadecimal number}] A number written in base=16 instead of base=10
as usual. (0 = \texttt{0}, 1 = \texttt{1}, \ldots{}, 9 = \texttt{9},
10 = \texttt{A}, \ldots{}, 15 = \texttt{F}, 16 = \texttt{10}, \ldots{}, 
31 = \texttt{1F}, \ldots
\end{itemize}
